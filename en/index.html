About

Making digital transformation accessible, fast and profitable

BALEREX DIGITAL is a digital services company being established in Abidjan. Our ambition: to become, within five years, a reference partner for the digital transformation of SMEs and SMIs across French-speaking West Africa.

We first focus our efforts on Côte d'Ivoire to build solid references, before expanding to Benin and then the UEMOA / ECOWAS area — a replicable model, largely deliverable remotely.
