About

Making digital transformation accessible, fast and profitable

BALEREX DIGITAL supports SMEs and SMIs across French-speaking West Africa in their transformation, combining the power of generative AI with the rigour of experts.

Our story

A clear five-year ambition

BALEREX DIGITAL is a digital services company being established in Abidjan. Our ambition: to become a reference partner for the digital transformation of SMEs and SMIs across French-speaking West Africa.

We first focus our efforts on Côte d'Ivoire to build solid references, before expanding to Benin and then the UEMOA / ECOWAS area — a replicable model, largely deliverable remotely.

Our conviction

Technology only has value if it serves concrete results. We put AI to work for productivity, but we always keep humans at the point of decision. AI accelerates; the expert stays accountable.

At a glance

  • Form SARL (OHADA)
  • Head office Abidjan, Côte d'Ivoire
  • Targets SMEs & SMIs — retail, industry, transport, rail
  • Expansion Benin, then West Africa
