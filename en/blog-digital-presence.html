Building a credible digital presence in a few weeks

For an SME, being online isn't enough: you have to be credible. Here's how we build, in a few weeks, a presence that establishes trust.

1. A clear editorial line

Before publishing, we define who we're talking to, about what and in what tone. This editorial line avoids ad-hoc content and gives the brand consistency.

2. A sustainable rhythm

Eight regular, polished posts beat thirty improvised ones. An editorial calendar plans production and smooths the workload.

3. Simple indicators

Reach, engagement, contacts generated: a few indicators are enough to steer and prove value. We measure, learn and adjust.
